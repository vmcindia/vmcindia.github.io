LIVE QUIZZES FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Live Quiz?

Live Quiz by Vidyamandir Classes is an interactive means of learning and checking how well you have learnt. Registration is free to Non VMC students. If you are a VMC student, you already have access to it.

How can I register for Live Quizzes?

As a Non VMC student, you just have to visit the Live Quiz website http://livequiz.vidyamandir.com and click on the Register link to register for the quiz. Registration and participation is absolutely free of cost.

I am not a VMC student. Can I join?

VMC Students already have access to Live Quiz and are participating in it. All Non VMC studens can participate in it for free.

Why should I do Live Quizzes?

Live Quizzes are an interactive way to learn and check how good you know a particular topic. In addition, you can win some goodies.

Vidyamandir Classes has launched Live Quizzes for students, so that learning can be enhanced using Gamification.

Students can register for free and start availing the benefits of curated content by some of the best teachers of VMC.
